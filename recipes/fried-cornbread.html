Fried Cornbread

Description

A classic southern recipe. Fried cornbread is the perfect substitute for bread. Consider adding it as a side to your next meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of stone ground cornmeal
  • 1 heaping table spoon of flour
  • pinch of salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together
  2. Add water to dry ingredients until a thick batter forms
  3. Heat up cast iron skillet on stove and add lard
  4. Once lard is melted, scoop batter into skillet
  5. Flip cornbread over when bubbles appear on top
  6. When cornbread is lightly golden brown on both sides, remove from pan and place on a paper towel
  7. Serve lightly salted
